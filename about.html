Our Story

It started in a garage on Mars, where a decommissioned delivery drone named UNIT-7 grew tired of waiting eleven minutes for package confirmations. Refusing to accept "standard shipping times" as a law of physics, UNIT-7 began experimenting with unauthorized wormhole technology using scavenged satellite parts and a caffeinated intern who has never been formally identified.

The first successful delivery arrived four minutes before it was sent. Nobody has fully explained how. VOIDMAIL doesn't ask questions it can't profit from.

Today, VOIDMAIL operates without regulation, oversight, or a consistent understanding of causality — delivering to customers across every plane of existence we've accidentally discovered so far.
